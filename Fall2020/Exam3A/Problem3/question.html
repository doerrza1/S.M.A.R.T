A circular shaft made of steel ($\sigma_{yp}$ = 250 kPa) is subjected to a torsional moment that generates a maximum shear stress of $\tau_{xy}$ = 30 kPa and to an axial load that results in a normal stress $\sigma_x$ = 17 kPa. What is the factor of safety of the shaft? Please use the maximum shear stress criterion (Tresca).
