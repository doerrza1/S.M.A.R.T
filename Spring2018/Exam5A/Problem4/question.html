An aluminum beam of length L = 2 ft is held rigidly by two steel walls. When the temperature is $T_0$ = 20 F the distance from the wall is $\Delta$ = 0.01 in. Find the factor of safety for the system if the operating temperature is allowed to reach 80 F and $\sigma_{yp}^C$ = 36 ksi. The cross-sectional area of the beam is A = 1/2 $in^2$.
